Frequently Asked Questions

My friends want to play soccer in your club, how much is the price?

Our club is focused on providing our customers high quality at low cost, the middle fields cost 50 dollars per hour and the larger one costs 100 dollars per hour, do not forget to check our web page to receive weekly promotions so you and your teammates can get a good discount. Oh, we almost forgot it, fresh water is on the house.

We have an under eight years old, does the space have a place for him?

For sure, our club is for all those soccer lovers with no exception, your little Messi will love our club. We have an outdoor television space with a restaurant for family and friends, we ensure you that no problem will come from your wonderful kid attending us, except becoming a brilliant star.

I do not play soccer but I want to visit your club, do I still need to pay to stay in the club?

Of course not, come and have your own first impression, our stylish restaurant will accommodate you and provide food and drinks if you want. Do not hold back, come and visit us, we are waiting for you.

What are your opening hours?

We are open from Monday to Saturday, from 8 a.m to 9 p.m, feel free to come!

Do I need to pay to park at your parking lot?

No, the space is free of charges for all of our customers who are enjoying one of our activies. Just show one of our receipts at the reception when you leave.

How do the coupons are used?

Copy the coupon code and introduce it in our reception so the discount can be applied to your bill.
