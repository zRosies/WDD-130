Frequently Asked Questions

I and my friends want to play soccer in your club, how much is the price?

Our club is focused on providing our customers high quality at low cost, the middle fields cost 50 dollars per hour and the larger one costs 100 dollars per hour, do not forget to check our web page to receive weekly promotions so you and your teammates can get a good discount.

I have an under eight years old, does the space have a place for him?

For sure, our club is for all those soccer lovers with no exception, your little Messi will love our club. We have an outdoor television space with a restaurant for family and friends, we ensure you that no problem will come from your wonderful kid attending us, except becoming a brilliant star.

I do not play soccer but I want to visit your club, do I still need to pay to stay in the club?

Of course not, come and have your own first impression, our stylish restaurant will accommodate you and provide food and drinks if you want. Do not hold back, come and visit us, we are waiting for you.

What are your opening hours?

Our club is open from Monday to Saturday, from 8 a.m to 9 p.m, feel free to come!

Are you kidding me! What's not great about whitewater rafting!...Let's describe a common day on the river. Arise in the soft light of the early morning after a night spent sleeping under a beautiful starry sky. Have a hearty warm breakfast of fresh blueberry pancakes or omelets to order, then pack your things and prepare for the day. The day is filled with some combination of swift water, rapids, calms, hikes, history, geology, stories, and more good food. We try to hit camp around 4 or 5, after setting up your cot you can relax with a good book or good company by the river, or explore around camp while your guides prepare your dinner...something perhaps like steak and potatoes, or grilled salmon...and dutch oven brownies...you won't forget the dutch oven brownies. After dinner it's more relaxing, and you might even be able to talk your guide into another story or two to send you off to bed under more stars than you thought were possible.
