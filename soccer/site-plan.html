Home page

Welcome to the Soccer Max website, our site is designed for the users to feel more interested to know about the club. Our club has four middle fields and a big one crossing three mid fields, our fields have the greenest and most well-maintained grass you have ever seen, using daily inner irrigation in the earth through micro pipes that flush water with particles of vitamins mixed with water to nourish the soil, giving the best conditions for our customers to play and have a real Champions League experience. Besides the soccer activities, our club provides amazing family and friends areas, our barbecue section has a breathtaking kitchen with a large grill, soft illumination, an oven, and an excellent environment to have an unforgettable experience with your mates. Last but not least, our club was designed not only for soccer players but also for soccer lovers, within our facilities there is a place with an outdoor television and restaurant, the place is incredibly illuminated with soft lamps and well organized with tables along the place. The outdoor section is one of our club's masterpieces, the place is favorable to start new friendships, watch your favorite soccer games on a large television with your family and mates, and celebrate your team's victory.
