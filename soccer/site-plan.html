Overview

Purpose

Our website's purpose is to increase the notability of our newest soccer club by displaying all its amazing facilities online such as its fields, barbecue section, and its room in which all of our customers can watch games together.

Audience

From young to elders, SoccerMax comes as a website to motivate our customers of all ages to unite together to play, watch, and have some fun with their families in our new soccer club. SoccerMax will be available for Desktop, Android, and IOS.
