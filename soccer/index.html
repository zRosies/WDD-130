Soccer Fields

Our fields have the greenest and most well-maintained grass you have ever seen, using daily inner irrigation in the earth through micro pipes that flush water with particles of vitamins mixed with water to nourish the soil, giving the best conditions for our customers to play and have a real Champions League experience.

Games Outdoor

The outdoor section is one of our club's masterpieces, the place is favorable to start new friendships, watch your favorite soccer games on a large television with your family and mates, and celebrate your team's victory.

Barbecue Area

Our club provides amazing family and friends areas, our barbecue section has a breathtaking kitchen with a large grill, soft illumination, an oven, and an excellent environment to have an unforgettable experience with your mates.
